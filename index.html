About

My name is Timofei Sapozhnikov, and I am a game designer, artist, and animator.

I am a Game Design student at Northeastern University with hands-on experience in creating art assets and managing video game projects. In the past, I participated in independent projects and video game jams, always responsible for the final aesthetic look of the product. I keenly work in team on all stages of the development and am always ready to take on new roles in smaller projects. Now, I am looking forward to connecting my past art skills with my desires to manage and produce immersive art in the form of videogames.
